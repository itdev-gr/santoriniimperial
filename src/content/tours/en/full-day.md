A 7-8 hour private day across the southern half of the island and ending at the most famous sunset in the Aegean. Six stops, one car, no logistics — just the version of Santorini you came to see.

### Akrotiri Lighthouse

Located on the most southwestern tip of the island, the Akrotiri Lighthouse is one of the most beautiful sites in Santorini and offers impressive views.

### Red Beach

Visit Santorini's most well-known beach, Red Beach — a rare natural phenomenon — and take magnificent pictures of the reddish cliffs and volcanic surroundings.

### Museum of Akrotiri

Visit the Akrotiri archaeological site — the most significant prehistoric settlement in the entire Mediterranean.

### Prophet Ilias Monastery

The highest spot on the island is the mountain of Prophet Ilias — 2,000 feet (600 meters) above sea level — where the homonymous monastery sits. Take the chance to admire the panoramic view of the island, the caldera, and the neighboring islands.

### Wine Tasting

If you want to taste the local wines, we can visit Santo Wines — or Art Space Winery, or any winery you prefer — where you'll learn the secrets of winemaking and enjoy the famous local varieties.

### Oia

We end at the famous town of Oia, nestled in the hillsides. Oia is unique in every way — iconic blue-domed churches, scenic pathways, stunning views of the caldera, and the most spectacular sunset on the island.
